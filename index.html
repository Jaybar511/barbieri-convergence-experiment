About the Author

John Barbieri (also known as Jay Barbieri) is a pioneering visionary in quantum theory, artificial intelligence, AGI, and ASI. He is most notably recognized for reshaping the music industry in the early 1990s by launching the first internet-based digital music download platform (J-Bird Records) —years before the iPod (Apple) or any mainstream hardware existed. Barbieri’s mind has always operated from a holistic, Occam’s Razor-inspired perspective—seeking the shortest, most elegant path to solving complex problems. Rather than thinking linearly, he sees entire systems at once, connecting ideas across time and domain. With major scientific breakthroughs in 2025, Barbieri began applying decades of original theoretical work to proven models in quantum mechanics and AI. This site is dedicated to sharing and publishing those ongoing explorations and open-source contributions.
