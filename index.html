Rather than thinking linearly, he sees entire systems at once, connecting ideas across time and domain. With major scientific breakthroughs in 2025, Barbieri began applying decades of original theoretical work to proven models in quantum mechanics and AI. This site is dedicated to sharing and publishing those ongoing explorations and open-source contributions.
